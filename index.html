Privacy Policy for Tank Radar

Effective Date: April 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how I, Damian Perera ("I", "me", or "my"), an individual developer based in Bavaria, Germany, collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Tank Radar iOS application ("the App"). I am committed to protecting your personal data and complying with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Data Controller

I am the Data Controller responsible for your personal information under the GDPR (Art. 4(7) GDPR). For general questions, bug reports, or feature requests, please open a ticket on GitHub. For privacy-specific enquiries or to exercise your rights under this policy, you may also reach me by email at: support@perera.io

Information I Collect and How I Use It

Tank Radar collects two categories of data: your device's geographical location (to provide core functionality) and anonymised usage analytics (to improve the App). Both are processed with care and in full respect of your privacy.

Location Data

  • I request your precise or approximate location via iOS system permissions.
  • This location data is used exclusively in real-time to query third-party APIs and display nearby gas stations.
  • Your location data is not linked to your identity, device ID, or Apple ID.
  • I process this data based on your explicit consent provided through the iOS location permission prompt, which you can revoke at any time in your device settings.

Analytics Data (Google Analytics for Firebase)

  • I use Google Analytics for Firebase to collect anonymised usage data to understand how the App is used and to improve its features and performance.
  • This data is collected only if you explicitly consent when prompted by the App.
  • The legal basis for processing analytics data is your explicit consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR).
  • You can withdraw this consent at any time via the App's settings or your iOS device settings.

No Personally Identifiable Information (PII)

Google's terms expressly prohibit sending any Personally Identifiable Information (PII) to Firebase. No names, email addresses, Apple IDs, or precise GPS coordinates are ever transmitted to Google Analytics.

Data Storage and Security

I do not operate backend servers that store your personal information.

  • Location data is processed temporarily in the device's memory to fulfil the API request and is never written to disk, transmitted to me, or logged anywhere.
  • Analytics data, if you have consented to it, is transmitted to and stored by Google on Firebase infrastructure in accordance with Google's security and data retention policies.
  • I do not transmit, log, or store your location history, searches, or any other personal data on my own servers.
  • Because I do not operate data stores myself, the risk of data breaches affecting your personal information through my systems is virtually eliminated.

Google's Data Retention

If you have consented to analytics, Google Firebase retains analytics data according to the retention period configured in the Firebase console. You can request deletion of your analytics data at any time by emailing me at support@perera.io and I will submit a deletion request via the Firebase User Deletion API on your behalf.

For more information about Google Firebase's deletion timeline: developers.google.com

Analytics and Tracking

Tank Radar uses Google Analytics for Firebase to collect anonymised usage data. This helps me understand which features are used, how the App performs across different devices and regions, and where to focus future improvements.

What is collected: App Instance ID, Identifier for Vendor (IDFV), device model and iOS version, App version, language, session duration, screen views, and in-app events (e.g. station viewed, map interaction). Approximate region (country/city level) is derived from your IP address - the IP address itself is not logged or stored by Google.

What is NOT collected: Precise GPS coordinates, your name, email, Apple ID, payment information, or any content you personally interact with. Google's terms prohibit the transmission of any Personally Identifiable Information to Firebase.

Consent Required Under GDPR

Analytics collection does not begin until you have explicitly consented via the in-app prompt. You may withdraw your consent at any time - analytics collection will stop immediately and no further data will be sent to Firebase.

How to opt out or withdraw consent:

  • iOS Settings: Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking and disable tracking for Tank Radar.
  • Advertising Identifier: Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising and enable "Limit Ad Tracking" to restrict the identifier used by Firebase.
  • Data Deletion: Open a support ticket or email me at support@perera.io to request deletion of any analytics data Firebase holds associated with your installation.
