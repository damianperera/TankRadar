✦ Features

Everything you need at the pump

Built for drivers who want the best price without the distraction.

🚗

CarPlay Integration

View nearby stations and prices directly on your CarPlay screen. Tap to get turn-by-turn directions to the cheapest station - hands-free and distraction-free.

💰

Find Cheaper Fuel Nearby

Set your search radius between 5 and 25 km and see stations sorted by price and distance. The best deal is always clearly highlighted at the top.

⏱️

On-Device ETA & Live Updates

Estimated travel time to each station is calculated directly on your device. Pull to refresh anytime to get the very latest prices.

🗺️

Your Favourite Maps App

Tap any station to open the route in Google Maps or Waze - or jump straight into CarPlay for a fully integrated experience.

🧳

Smart for Road Trips

Tank Radar auto-updates fuel types and prices as you cross borders, so you always see what's relevant - no matter which country you're driving through.

🔄

One-Tap Fuel Type Change

Picked up a rental with a different engine? Switch your preferred fuel type instantly from the fuel selector dropdown - no digging through menus. Always see the right prices for whatever you're driving today.
